Welcome to NFT Raffle

Follow the instructions below to use our platform:

  1. Click the "Connect Wallet" button to connect your Solana wallet.
  2. Once connected, select an NFT from your wallet to create a raffle.
  3. Set the ticket price and end time for your raffle.
  4. Click "Create Raffle" to start your raffle.
  5. Share your raffle link using the "Share Raffle Link" button.
  6. Check the "Active Raffles" and "Past Raffles" sections to see ongoing and past raffles.
